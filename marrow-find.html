The Challenge

Manual bone marrow diagnosis is a grueling process, often taking 45–90 minutes per patient. Beyond the time cost, there exists a significant 20% inter-observer variability, where different pathologists may reach different conclusions based on the same slide.

The Solution

Marrow-Find is an end-to-end automated ALL leukaemia detection pipeline. Leveraging custom vision architectures, it processes complex cytological samples to deliver comprehensive PDF reports and diagnostic heatmaps in just 2–3 seconds.
